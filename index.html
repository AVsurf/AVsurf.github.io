Overview



Model Framework

We introduce AV-Surf , a novel framework for binaural audio generation designed to achieve two key objectives: (i) integration of surface and 3D geometry priors into position-aware frequency embedding using a transformer-based architecture and (ii) to develop spectral feature enhancing module by leveraging a ConvNeXt-based structure.
